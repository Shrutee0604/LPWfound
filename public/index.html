language specifications

why should learn japanese language?

Increased career prospects

You may have a better chance of getting hired if you know a second language. This will also allow you to negotiate higher salaries.

communication with over 125 million speakers

As per the 2022 Ethnologue research, over 125 million people speak Japanese as their mother tongue or first language. This makes Japanese the 9th most spoken native language in the world. Japanese can help you speak with nearly 130 million more people than you could not before and connect on a deeper level.

A gateway to an incomparable cultural universe

Japanese culture is unique and a perfect mixture of tradition and modernity. The ability to speak Japanese opens a window into one of the worldâ€™s most fascinating, multicolored, and ancient civilizations.

Job opportunities in Japanese companies

With the global market expansion and globalization, many Japanese companies have set up their corporate offices or local offices in different countries. They are doing it to expand business and skill-building.

Japanese will set you apart from the crowd

When you learn a less-famous language like Japanese, you will have something rewarding to add to your resume. To add more value to your CV, you can take Japanese tests like JLPT or NAT test.

The international spread of the language

Japanese technology and products hold a good grip on the global market. As a result, Japanese is an internationally acknowledged and recognized language. Learning Japanese can be like adding a feather to the hat in the global market as it enables communication with the big Japanese players.
